NYCShells

Patent- pending technology that starts off with a multifunctional sole

NYCShells is a shoe design concept that allows the consumer to create numerous looks with one pair of shoe soles. The soles have a proprietary spring lock mechanism that runs around the edge of the sole. These spring locks retract and lock in place by pressing the silver buttons on each side of the sole. When an upper attachment, referred to as a shell, is attached, it is locked into place once the buttons are released. To remove, simply press the buttons again, the locks will retract, and the upper shell will be released. The spring lock mechanism will replace the purpose of glue in current shoes, which is to lock the upper shells to the soles.
